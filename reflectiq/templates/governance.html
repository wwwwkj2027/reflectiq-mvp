🔒 Governance & Data Use

How ReflectIQ uses your data

This page explains what information is collected, how it is separated, and the governance rules that protect students and inform faculty.

🪪
Completion Tracking — Identifiable
When you submit a reflection, your name and student ID are recorded in a separate completion log. This confirms that you participated in the session activity.
  • Used only for attendance and completion tracking
  • Visible to faculty on the Completion Status page
  • Never linked to your reflection answers
  • Never used for grading or ranking
🧠
Reflection Analysis — Anonymized
Your three reflection answers (Q1, Q2, Q3) are stored in a separate anonymous table with no name, student ID, or any identifying information attached.
  • Classified by AI into learning signals only
  • Stored without any student identifier
  • Cannot be traced back to you
  • Used only for aggregated class-level pattern analysis
📊
Faculty Dashboard — Aggregated Only
The faculty learning signals dashboard shows only aggregated patterns. No individual student response is ever displayed.
  • Clusters with fewer than 3 responses are suppressed for privacy
  • No individual attribution — ever
  • AI signals require faculty review before any action
  • Faculty decision remains final on all instructional matters
